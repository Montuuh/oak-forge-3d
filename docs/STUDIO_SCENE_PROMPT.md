# Prompt — referencia de escena (Nano Banana 2 / externo)

Genera un set **vacío** (sin producto) y súbelo como `public/images/studio/default.jpg` o desde admin → **Subir escena** (v7).

## Positivo

```
Professional e-commerce product photography studio, empty set, no objects on the desk.

Environment: continuous solid oak wood desk, natural warm oak grain, clean and uncluttered. The tabletop must NOT end in frame — it extends beyond the left, right, and bottom edges as an endless surface; no visible front edge, lip, or drop-off of the desk.

Camera to desk: moderate diagonal angle (roughly 25–35° from parallel to the surface), eye level, 50mm lens look, f/2.8 shallow depth of field. The desk plane recedes diagonally into the scene, not a flat bird's-eye nor a straight-on wall shot.

Behind the desk: soft neutral off-white gotelé textured wall (subtle raised pattern), seamless where desk meets wall.

Lighting: soft natural daylight from the left, ~45° elevation, neutral 5500K, soft diffuse shadow on the desk toward the right, no harsh hotspots.

Style: hyper-realistic photograph, premium minimalist catalog backdrop, high detail wood and wall texture.

CRITICAL: completely empty desk — no figurines, plants, props, hands, text, or logos.
```

## Negativo

```
product, figurine, pokemon, pokeball, toy, plant, props, hands, person, text, watermark, logo,
visible end of table, desk edge toward camera, truncated desk, table corner cut off, short tabletop,
straight-on flat desk, top-down desk, bird's eye, dutch angle,
neon, colored gels, wrong wood, flat painted wall, brick, window, clutter,
CGI, cartoon, oversaturated HDR
```

## Tras generar

Relación 4:3 o 3:2, máxima resolución. Elige la variante donde la mesa **no termina** en el encuadre y el ángulo a la superficie sea claramente **diagonal**.
